Safety control

Safety Control is a mobile app that monitors if your employees remember to wear personal protective equipment.

Artificial intelligence technology is used to analyze data from video. This helps to detect objects at the production site and people when they enter a dangerous zone to prevent injuries.

When a worker approaches the machine, AI technology checks whether this person is wearing personal protective equipment or not. If not, Safety Control app sends a warning requiring staff to put on a protective helmet.

At the same time, Safety Control app takes a photo of the person who neglects safety protocols. The image is saved in Safety Control Odoo, where managers can observe those who violate safety rules and contact them directly.
